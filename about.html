About Me

Kayla Julianna Carabes (b. 2002 Houston, TX) is a multidisciplinary artist whose work explores Painting and performance art. Nature is a key influence in her creations, with its colors, textures and forms serving as the foundation for her otherworldly landscapes and dreamlike scenes. The natural environments act as a platform from which she can recount memories and archive the fleeting moment. Kayla is currently earning a BFA in Painting from Rhode Island School Of Design in Providence, RI.
